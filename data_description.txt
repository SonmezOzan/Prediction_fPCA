Data description
================

We have 1/2 hour average values from 01.10.2010 till 31.03.2011

Date:  clear
Time:  clear
NR:    intraday number 1-48
PM GR: PM10 at station Graz-Mitte (city center)
PM JU: PM10 at station Judendorf-Sued (suburb)
T GR:  Temperature at Graz Mitte (350 m)
T KL:  Temperature at Kalkleiten (700 m)

There were only very few missing values, we used ad hoc
methods to replace them by estimates (neighboring values
or interpolation ...)
